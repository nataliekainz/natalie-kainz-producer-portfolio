My Work

INVESTIGATIVE FEATURE | How NDAs keep AI data center details hidden from Americans

I pitched, wrote and produced this investigative feature story about the use of non-disclosure agreements in data center development across the United States. It analyses how tech companies use secrecy agreements with local governments to keep communities from knowing who is building in their backyards. It was the result of a three month investigation I did for NBCNews.com on the same topic which involved filing 50 public records requests across 15 states.

About Me

Natalie's reporting experience is split across her hometown Hong Kong and her current home in New York. She has worked for CNN International Productions, NBC News, The South China Morning Post, and the New Haven Independent. She was also the Multimedia Managing Editor of the Yale Daily News from 2021-2022. She's passionate about reporting on international affairs, culture, and technology.
